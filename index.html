Welcome to The Forsaken Labyrinth game!

In 'The Forsaken Labyrinth', plunge into the depths of a dark and treacherous castle, where shadows conceal ancient secrets and traps lie in wait. Navigate through winding corridors and eerie chambers, collecting lost artifacts while evading deadly hazards. With each step, unravel the mysteries of this forsaken realm and strive to escape its sinister grasp. Are you brave enough to face the labyrinth's challenges and emerge victorious?

Controls

Up, Left and Right keys to move

Down key to interact with platforms and ladders

Right Click to teleport

Left click to collect items

Features

1. Teleportation Mechanics: Harness the power of teleportation to navigate through intricate levels and outmaneuver your foes.

2. Potion Collection: Gather health and teleportation potions strategically placed throughout the labyrinth to survive and thrive.

3. Dynamic Lighting: Explore the depths of darkness with an immersive lighting system that sets the mood and adds an extra layer of challenge.

4. Ingenious Traps: Encounter cunning traps designed to test your reflexes and intellect as you traverse the treacherous terrain.

5. Interactive Environments: Interact with one-way collision platforms and ladders, offering new pathways and strategic advantages in your quest for escape.

6. Atmospheric Ambiance: Immerse yourself in a hauntingly atmospheric world, where every shadow conceals secrets and dangers alike.

Requirements

Internet connection and a web browser. This is a PC game and can be played only in PC.
